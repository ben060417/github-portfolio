Methodology & Disclaimers

This dashboard was built by Benjamin Min as a personal research project aggregating publicly available commercial real estate market data from institutional brokerages. It is not affiliated with any data provider.

Data Sourcing

Every metric is sourced from a specific named report. The source is shown on each metric card. Where a figure could not be directly verified from a published report, it has been removed rather than estimated. Approximate figures (~) reflect published ranges from executive summaries.

Geographic Scope

London figures distinguish between (1) Central London office market per Cushman & Wakefield London MarketBeat, and (2) UK Regional & South East offices per Cushman & Wakefield Regional MarketBeat. These are two separate reports covering different geographies and are not interchangeable.

Transaction Data

All transactions verified via at least one of: REIT press releases, CoStar News, The Real Deal, Commercial Property Executive, or broker research. No fabricated deal data is included. Where deal terms are undisclosed, this is stated explicitly.

Currency Conversions

KRW and JPY investment volumes converted at approximate exchange rates current at time of publication. Indicative only.

Limitations

  • Cap rate data for non-US markets reflects prime CBD assets; may not represent broader market averages.
  • This dashboard does not constitute investment advice.
  • Data reflects conditions as of Q1 2026 (March 31, 2026) unless noted otherwise.
